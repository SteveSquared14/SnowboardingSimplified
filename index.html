Background

From the age of 11 or 12 I would watch the Winter X Games every year when it was on TV, for those that are unfamiliar the Winter X Games are basically the Olympics of the winter actions sports world where sports like Skiing, Snowboarding & Snowmobile compete across multiple different disciplines in an attempt take home the gold medal.

Over the next couple of years I became an avid fan and watched the Winter X Games religiously which resulted in my parents surprising me with snowboarding lessons for my 14th birthday. I learned my skills at the Chill Factore based in Trafford, Manchester.

I have been lucky enough to go on 9 winter holidays since then to countries including: Andorra, France, Italy, Spain & Austria
